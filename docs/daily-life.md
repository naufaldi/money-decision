# Daily Life Expenses Breakdown for Indonesia

This document provides comprehensive categories and recommended allocations for daily life expenses in Indonesia, based on cost of living data and local spending patterns.

## Overview

The average monthly net salary in Indonesia is approximately **5,000,000 Rp**. A single person's monthly expenses (excluding rent) average around **7,264,223 Rp**, while a family of four spends approximately **25,839,498 Rp** (excluding rent).
