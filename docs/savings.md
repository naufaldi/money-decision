# Savings Guidance

This document provides guidance on how to allocate and manage your savings based on the Money Decision app recommendations.

## Emergency Fund Recommendations

| Stage | Target Amount | Where to Keep |
|-------|---------------|---------------|
| **Starter** | 1 month expenses | High-Yield Savings Account |
| **Basic** | 3 months expenses | High-Yield Savings Account |
| **Standard** | 6 months expenses | High-Yield Savings Account |
| **Strong** | 12 months expenses | Split: 50% HYSA, 50% Money Market |

## Savings Options Comparison

| Option | Risk | Return | Liquidity | Best For |
|--------|------|--------|-----------|----------|
| **High-Yield Savings Account** | Very Low | 3-5% APY | High | Emergency fund |
| **Money Market Account** | Very Low | 3-5% APY | High | Short-term savings |
| **Certificates of Deposit (CD)** | Very Low | 4-5% APY | Medium | Fixed-term goals |
| **Treasury Bonds** | Very Low | 4-5% | Medium | Conservative investors |
| **Digital Wallet (OVO, DANA, etc.)** | Low | 1-3% | Very High | Daily spending buffer |

## Breakdown Categories

The app splits savings into three categories:

### 1. Emergency Fund (50% of savings)

Purpose: 3-6 months of essential expenses for unexpected events

Where to keep: High-Yield Savings Account (3-5% APY) - Most liquid and safe

This is your financial safety net for:
- Job loss
- Medical emergencies
- Home/vehicle repairs
- Any unexpected major expense

### 2. Short-term Goals (30% of savings)

Purpose: Things you want to achieve within 1 year

Where to keep: Money Market Account or Digital Wallet (OVO, DANA) - Easy access

Examples:
- Vacation
- Gadgets/electronics
- Online courses
- Weekend trips
- Small purchases

### 3. Medium-term Goals (20% of savings)

Purpose: Larger objectives over 1-5 years

Where to keep: Certificates of Deposit (CD) or Treasury Bonds (4-5% return)

Examples:
- Education/courses
- Vehicle purchase
- Wedding
- Down payment
- Business investment

## Tips

1. **Automate your savings**: Set up automatic transfers on payday
2. **Keep emergency fund liquid**: Don't lock it in long-term investments
3. **Match account to goal**: Use appropriate accounts for each savings type
4. **Review regularly**: Adjust percentages based on life changes
5. **Start small**: Even Rp 500,000 monthly builds up over time
